Recommended beginner fangames

This page will hopefully recommend fun, easy fangames to play if you haven't played any before! (What are fangames?)

To start, here are some general recommendations, many of which are classified as "adventure games":

I wanna be the 8possible

A spiritual journey through 100+ floors

I wanna be the Zero Game

A fun romp with lots of variety

I wanna be the Animus

Dream-like quest

If you're looking specifically for troll games, which are overloaded with clever and hilarious traps, here you go:

I wanna wanna

A short but super-sweet troll game

If you're looking specifically for needle games, which focus on just intricate, precise spike jumps, here you go:

I wanna be the MU-GEEEEEEEEE

Nice needle with retro graphics

If you're looking specifically for avoidance games, in which you dodge bullet barrages in sync with music, here you go:

For My Valentine

Romantic and epic avoidance

I wanna use Snow Magic

Cute and well-made avoidance

Recommendations beyond this page

This page is only a starting point; there are so many fangames to discover! Here are a few ways to find more fangames:

- Check out curated lists by Geezer, Sunbla & Zero, Paragus, Denferok.
- Browse Delicious Fruit and search by rating, difficulty, tags, and more.
- Ask friends in the community for recommendations based on what you like.

In closing
